A critical look at the article using the following parameter :

  • Data & Abstractions

    • Laughter is complex. In real life it can be hard to tell the real from the fake. Online, it is even harder, with fewer visual cues and a wider range of tools to express laughter. As internet language develops, new forms of laughter spread.This article takes reddit comments and show the way users use different words to express laughter and more.
